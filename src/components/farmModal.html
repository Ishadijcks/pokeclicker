Farm

In the farm you can plant and harvest Berries.

Each Berry type takes a certain amount of time to grow. Once fully grown, the Berry plant can be clicked to harvest the Berries.

Farm Tokens can be gained by harvesting Berries.

After a while, the Berry plant will wither, dropping only a fraction of the Berries.

Additional plots can be unlocked by spending Berries.

Mutations

Berries have the chance to mutate into other Berries based on certain conditions.

The Kanto Berry Master will give you daily hints to what these conditions may be.

Mulches

Mulches can be bought in later regions from Berry Masters. They can be used on plots for different effects:

Boost Mulch Increases the growth rate of Berry plants.
Rich Mulch Increases the number of Berries harvested.
Surprise Mulch Increases the chances of mutations.
Amaze Mulch A weaker version of all three affects.

Berry Shovel

If you want to remove a Berry plant before it is fully grown, you can use a Berry Shovel to uproot it.

Berry Shovels can be bought in later regions from Berry Masters.

Berry Trades

Berries can be traded with Berry Masters for other items
